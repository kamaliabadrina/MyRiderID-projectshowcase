MyRiderID

Smart QR Code Management System

A comprehensive digital identity solution for motorcycle riders, enabling seamless QR code management, emergency contact access, and enhanced safety features through modern web technology.

Key Features

🏍️

Multi-Vehicle Management

Centralized dashboard allowing riders to manage multiple QR codes for different vehicles under one account, improving scalability and user experience.

👤

Personalized Profiles

Comprehensive rider profiles with personal information, emergency contacts, medical conditions, and vehicle details for enhanced safety.

🚨

Emergency Response

Quick access to critical information during emergencies, including medical conditions, blood type, and emergency contacts for first responders.

📱

Modern Interface

Intuitive, responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices, providing an optimal user experience for both riders and emergency personnel.
